Payment Methods

Accept Card Payments

Paid makes it very quick and easy for small and medium-sized merchants, eCommerce sites and marketplaces to accept card payments. Paid’s pre-built integrations link seamlessly with your checkout page to ensure a quick and secure checkout flow for your customers.

Our responsive design displays checkout page correctly on all the devices. And our secure checkout builds confidence and increases conversions saving you cost and time to accepting payments.

Request to Pay (R2P):

No need for a website

Paid lets you create a Request to Pay (R2P) link on the Paid's Merchant Portal in just a few steps—give it a name, give it a price, and save. And it’s ready to share.

Simply share the link directly with your customers by email, Instant Messenger or SMS so they can proceed with your payment request.

Customers click through and get taken to a simple checkout page where they can make a seamless and secure payment. R2P links are the easiest and fastest way to get paid and can be used across various customer communication channels.
